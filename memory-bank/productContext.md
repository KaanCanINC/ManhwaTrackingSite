# Product Context

## Problem
User tracks a very large reading list with hundreds of open tabs and loses track of what is planned, active, or completed.

## Experience Targets
- Fast add/update for chapter progress and notes.
- Clear filtering for reading/completed/plan_to_read and custom flags.
- Easy source-link access for TR and EN sites.
- Reliable local backup and restore behavior for self-hosted usage.

## Non-Goals for v1
- Multi-user auth.
- Google Drive backup integration.
